Black Iris

Black Iris is a dynamic action game that pulls you into the world of monsters governed by one, simple rule – the rarer your eye color is, the higher in society you are.

Join in for the adventure of a lifetime with the seasoned warrior hoping to reclaim his eye – Black Iris – that was stolen by a powerful, evil warlock.

Slash through hordes of monsters on your way to the top of the warlock’s mighty tower. Discover new levels, skins, and hone your skills while trying to obtain the highest possible score in every level – don’t let your friends beat yourself to it!

See you in the tower!
